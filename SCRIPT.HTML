Their Journey

How it all began

From a Deepavali kitchen in Puchong to late-night lockdown calls and a Bird Park proposal.

Deepavali in Puchong

The first conversation

Arun and Divya first met at a friend’s Deepavali open house in Puchong—one of those crowded, noisy Malaysian houses where kids run everywhere and aunties guard the murukku tins. In the middle of the chaos, they ended up stuck in the kitchen, refilling drinks and talking like they’d known each other for years.

Lockdown & Late‑Night Calls

When it became serious

During the lockdown, KL and Rawang felt further apart than ever. Still, every night, there was a video call—sometimes with words, sometimes with comfortable silence. On one of those nights, Divya fell asleep studying on call and woke up at 4 a.m. to see Arun still there, half asleep, laptop glowing. “I didn’t want you to wake up alone,” he said.

Rain, Scooters & Teh Tarik

Their favourite memory

On a trip to Penang, a sudden downpour soaked them on a rented scooter near Batu Ferringhi. They ducked into a tiny roadside stall, dripping wet, sharing hot teh tarik and laughing at how ridiculous they looked. No plan, no schedule—just two people realising they could handle anything together.

Kuala Lumpur Bird Park

The proposal

Surrounded by parrots and macaws at the KL Bird Park, Arun handed Divya a small photo album filled with five years of memories. On the last page, a question: “Will you keep collecting moments with me?” Before she could answer, a nearby bird squawked so loudly that everyone turned to watch her say yes.

Temple Ceremony

Sacred Moments

A South Indian Hindu wedding, honouring family, culture, and the promises made before the sacred fire.

Maalai Maatral

Exchanging garlands

Arun and Divya will exchange flower garlands, a playful yet symbolic moment where both families watch them choose each other—literally and spiritually.

Thaali Tying

The golden thread

In the presence of the agni (sacred fire), Arun will tie the thaali around Divya’s neck, marking the heart of the ceremony—where their promises become visible to everyone they love.

Saptapadi

Seven sacred steps

Together, they will take seven steps, each one representing a shared vow—to support, protect, cherish, grow, and walk through life side by side.

Blessings & Aashirwad

From both families

Parents, elders, and loved ones will bless the couple, weaving Arun and Divya’s story into the bigger story of both families.
